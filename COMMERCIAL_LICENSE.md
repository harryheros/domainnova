## What Requires a Commercial License

The following uses require explicit prior written authorization from the author:

- **SaaS deployment** — hosting DomainNova as part of a paid or freemium service
- **API resale** — exposing DomainNova data or functionality via a commercial API
- **OEM / white-label** — bundling DomainNova into a product sold to third parties
- **Enterprise internal use** — deploying DomainNova within a commercial organization beyond personal or research use
- **Redistribution** — redistributing DomainNova data or outputs as part of a paid product or service
- **Integration into paid products** — embedding DomainNova into software or services that generate revenue

---

## What Does Not Require a Commercial License

- Personal, non-commercial use
- Academic research and education
- Open-source projects under a compatible non-commercial license
- Evaluation and testing (non-production)
